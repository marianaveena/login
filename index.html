How can I book a courier with Delhivery?

You can book an order on our website or Android or iOS app. If you want to courier as a business, you can sign up for Delhivery One and start shipping instantly.

Why should I courier with Delhivery?

Free pickup from doorstep, with same day pick-up available. Prices start as low as Rs. 90. Ship upto 50kgs.

Does Delhivery pack my parcel?

No, Delhivery does not provide packaging services. Customers must pack the contents of their parcel securely, and write the AWB number on the parcel, before the pickup date. AWB number is generated after booking a pickup, and can be found along with your booking details.

What is Delhivery Protect, and why should I opt for it?

Delhivery Protect offers coverage against loss or damage of parcels.
